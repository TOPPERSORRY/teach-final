Beutiful Lines on Teachers

1) A teacher is a person who helps children and other persons to learn and progress in life.

2) A teacher takes the responsibility of shaping the lives and career of his/her students.

3) They feel pride and honour to direct their students on the right path of the life.

4) Teachers always award their students for good things and punish them for evil things.

5) They spend their whole life in giving quality education to their students.

6) Teachers teach their students to differentiate among good and bad things of life.

7) A teacher understands this fact that not all the students are equally capable and gives special attention to the weaker ones.

8) A good teacher makes learning process easy and interesting for the students.

9) The good impression of a teacher makes them a role model for the students and helps them to become a better individual.
